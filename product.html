The Cost of LPG: 12 KG, 35 KG LPG Cylinder Price in Bangladesh

12 KG Gas Cylinder Price in BD

If you ever went to buy or refill an LPG cylinder, the first size that you spotted must be 12 kg cylinders. These are produced in a compact shape keeping household use in mind. Thus, these are easy to use and move around. As of September 2021, the 12 kg cylinder refills are sold by the retailers at around BDT 1100-1200 depending on the location. And if you are buying LPG for the first time, a 12 kg package will cost you around BDT 2000-2100.

35 KG LPG Cylinder Price In Bangladesh

This cylinder size is mostly used at the commercial level. As the need for LPG is higher in the commercial arena than in households, a 35 kg cylinder is a good choice. It has relatively less hassle of refilling due to higher capacity. For September 2021, a 35 kg LPG cylinder price in Bangladesh is sitting around at BDT 3000-3200, if you need a refill. The price is well inside the purchasing power of small and medium businesses as they are the main target for this cylinder size. However, if you need to purchase a 35 kg LPG package, you have to pay around BDT 4600-4800.

LPG In High Demand!

LPG is becoming increasingly common for both domestic and industrial use as the era of pipelined gas draws to a close. Many consumers and businesses have been compelled to shift to LPG because of the inconsistent gas supply. Despite the fact that demand for LPG has surged in recent years. The supply hasn't shown itself to be sufficient. The current LPG demand is roughly in the neighborhood of 1 million tons. Last year, there were also 1 million LPGs available overall. There is an overstock situation right now rather than a scarcity on the market. Being heavily reliant on imports, the LPG industry is very challenging to regulate. Even with price increases, there is still a rising demand for LPG. Many businesses and homes have shifted entirely to LPG. So, there are now much more LP gas dealerships in Bangladesh. LPG providers are also considering the supply of industrial gas. Additionally, the government supports emerging LPG businesses. Therefore, it is anticipated that industrial gas suppliers and LPG companies would continue to multiply in Bangladesh during the next years.

Safety

Safety is the utmost priority while deciding on an LP gas dealership in Bangladesh. So, never compromise with the safety of the cylinders. Before choosing a supplier you must tap into their LPG production procedure. Many companies are still lagging in using advanced technologies to ensure safety. You should check if the company maintains international standards while manufacturing the cylinders or not. For 12 kg cylinders, DOT 4BA 240 and 35 kg cylinders, DOT 4BW 240 specification standards should be followed. The raw materials used for manufacturing the cylinders should be scrutinized as well. The steel materials should be imported from reputed producers to ensure longevity. Also, the outer body thickness should be regulated. Remember to check the valve quality as well as the anti-rust coating the manufacturers are using.
